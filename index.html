🎯 How to Play WordBytes

  1. Guess the 6-letter word in 6 tries.
  2. Type letters using the on-screen keyboard.
  3. Press ENTER to submit.
  4. Letters will turn:
    • 🟦 Blue = Right letter, right position
    • 🟨 Yellow = Right letter, wrong position
    • ⬛ Gray = Letter not in word
  5. Use a hint (watch ad) if stuck!
  6. Share your result and challenge friends!
